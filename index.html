Our Approach

Principled, patient, and criteria-driven.

True North operates with a clear investment philosophy: we own businesses for the long term. We are not optimising for a near-term exit. We are not bound by fund timelines, outside mandates, or third-party capital. Every decision is governed by what is right for our portfolio over a multi-year horizon.

Our approach combines the analytical discipline of private equity with the strategic patience of permanent ownership. We evaluate carefully, we act with conviction, and we stay committed to the businesses we acquire.

I

Long-term ownership

We acquire businesses to own and operate them — not to flip them. Our holding mindset shapes how we evaluate deals, how we support management, and how we measure success.

II

Internal capital advantage

We deploy our own capital. There are no outside investors to satisfy, no fundraising cycles to manage. This gives us the freedom to move thoughtfully and close with certainty.

III

Rigorous underwriting

Every opportunity is evaluated against clear criteria: earnings quality, business model durability, competitive positioning, management depth, and long-term growth potential.

IV

Confidential and respectful

Business discussions are handled with the utmost discretion. Whether or not a transaction results, we respect the trust that owners and advisors place in us during the process.

V

Marketable securities

Disciplined allocation to public equity and fixed income markets as part of a diversified, long-term investment portfolio.

Acquisitions

We are actively looking to acquire.

True North is an active acquirer. We are not a passive investor and we are not a short-term financial buyer. We look for businesses with clear fundamentals, reliable operations, and the potential for long-term growth — businesses we can own, improve, and build over time.

Our acquisition process is private, selective, and criteria-driven. Each opportunity is reviewed carefully on its merits. If you are a business owner considering a transition, or an advisor with a relevant mandate, we welcome a confidential conversation.

Acquisition criteria

What we look for Detail
Geography United States and select international markets — with a focus on established commercial and industrial businesses.
Business stage Established, operating businesses with a clear track record of revenue and profitability.
Industries Commercial and industrial sectors with durable demand and defensible positioning.
What we look for Clear revenue history · Strong or developable management · Opportunities for improvement or growth · Reasonable valuation expectations · A business model that can be understood and managed responsibly.
Deal structure Full acquisitions preferred. Strategic partnerships considered where well-structured and aligned.
Management We value capable management teams already in place. We are owners, not operators.

  • Operators open to a strategic partnership with a long-term minded acquirer
  • M&A advisors and brokers representing acquisition mandates in our areas of interest

About Us

Independent. Long-term. Privately held.

True North Investment is a private investment office with a clear mandate: acquire, own, and build businesses for the long term. We operate independently, deploying our own capital without outside investors or public mandates.

True North serves as the primary management vehicle for international business development and acquisition activity. Where our broader activities include educational development within Kuwait, True North is specifically focused on commercial and investment activity across international, non-educational sectors.

True North also serves as the principal vehicle for family investment office functions, including long-term capital stewardship, estate planning coordination, and the management of a multi-jurisdictional investment portfolio.

We are not a fund. We are not seeking capital. We are a disciplined, experienced team with the resources and the conviction to acquire good businesses and build them thoughtfully over time.
